Sadly my time at university is slowly coming to an end, and I have to think about getting a job. It's the end of an era, sure, but I'm looking forward to the challenge of working in a new environment, and I'm also looking forward to not eating pasta every night.

Another thing I'm looking forward to is having some extra money to save and invest. I know that's weird, but that's just the sort of person I am.

I created [this spreadsheet](http://www.megaupload.com/?d=TL6WC0Y8) to calculate how much money you can make if you start investing early. Play around with the monthly amount and the rate of return to see what happens.You can download it [here](http://www.megaupload.com/?d=TL6WC0Y8).

Just from this spreadsheet, you can see the ridiculous benefits of investing early. Compound interest is so much fun.And a little fact: if you want to be a millionaire, the easiest way to do it is to invest £700 per month in the stock market. If you get an 8% return (the historical return rate of the S&P 500), you'll have £1million in just under 30 years.

Beats playing the lottery.
